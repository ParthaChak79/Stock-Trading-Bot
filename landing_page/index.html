The AI Wealth Builder strategy

Rules-based entries, pre-defined exits

Ported directly from the strategy's canonical TradingView Pine Script, and backtested per stock before it's added to the watchlist.

ENTRY

Both conditions must be true

  • ✓MACD cooldown — the histogram falls within a defined min/max range, filtering out signals that are too hot or too cold.
  • ✓SMA-50 trend filter — price must trade above its 50-day moving average by a minimum threshold.
  • ✓One position per stock — no pyramiding into an existing trade.
EXIT

Whichever level is hit first

  • ✓Take-profit — a fixed % target above entry, tuned per stock.
  • ✓Stop-loss — a fixed % floor below entry to cap downside.
  • ✓Trailing stop — dormant until the trade's high reaches the activation level, then steps once to lock in gains and holds there.
