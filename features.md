# Features

What you get as a subscriber to the AI Wealth Builder Telegram alerts.

## 1. Automated Buy & Sell Trade Alerts

- Get a **Buy Alert** the moment our strategy identifies a high-probability entry on one of your tracked NSE stocks — with entry price, target price, stop-loss, and a trailing-stop plan, all pre-calculated for that specific stock.
- Get a **Sell Alert** automatically when a position closes — whether it hit its target, its stop-loss, or locked in gains via the trailing stop — along with the exact profit/loss on the trade.
- Every alert includes the 2 most recent news headlines for that stock, each tagged with a sentiment read (positive/negative/neutral), so you have context the moment you see the signal.
- Disciplined risk management is built in: one position per stock at a time, with a predefined exit plan set the moment you enter — no emotional decision-making required.

## 2. Portfolio Tracking

- Your active positions and full trade history are tracked automatically — no spreadsheets required.
- A **Weekly Portfolio Report** lands every Friday afternoon: current holdings with live profit/loss, plus a look back at everything closed in the last 3 months.
- Miss an alert? You'll get a same-message reminder the next morning so nothing slips through.

## 3. Breaking News Alerts

- Independent of trade signals, you're notified in real time whenever fresh, market-moving news breaks on any of your tracked stocks.
- Each headline is sentiment-tagged so you can gauge the tone at a glance, and duplicate/near-identical stories are filtered out so you're not alerted twice for the same news.

## 4. Earnings Surprise Alerts

- The moment a tracked company files its quarterly results, you get an alert comparing actual Revenue, Net Profit, and EPS against analyst expectations.
- Each metric is labeled **Beat / Miss / In-line**, so you immediately know whether the quarter surprised to the upside or downside — no need to dig through a results PDF yourself.

## 5. Market Crash Protection Alert

- If the broader market suddenly drops sharply intraday, you get an immediate heads-up — a prompt to review your open positions before things move further, rather than finding out after the fact.

## 6. Daily Pre-Market Brief

Delivered every trading morning before the market opens, so you can plan your day in under a minute:

- **GIFT Nifty setup** — how India is expected to open
- **Global cues** — how major world markets (Asia, US) closed/are trading
- **Commodities & currency** — crude oil, gold, and the rupee
- **Institutional money flow** — what big domestic and foreign investors did yesterday, including whether they're on a sustained buying or selling streak
- **Volatility gauge** — a read on how nervous or calm the market is, with a callout when it spikes
- **Yesterday's close** — where the index ended and how broad the move was (advances vs. declines)
- **Overall bias** — a simple positive/neutral/cautious read to frame your day

## 7. Continuously Curated Stock Universe

The stocks you get alerts on aren't a static, hand-picked list — they're drawn from a shortlist of high-quality, growing businesses that's re-screened every week.

- A **30-parameter fundamental scoring engine** ranks NSE companies across five dimensions — financial quality, valuation, business strength, governance, and momentum — and only admits those that clear a strict quality bar.
- Each company's **exposure to AI and automation** is scored as part of that model — a structural tailwind for some businesses, a risk for others — so the shortlist favours companies positioned to benefit rather than be disrupted.
- Weak sectors and stagnant-growth businesses are **filtered out entirely** before scoring, keeping the focus on durable compounders.
- The list **refreshes on a rolling basis**: existing names are re-checked against fresh data and drop off if their quality decays, while genuinely new qualifying businesses rise onto the shortlist — so the watchlist behind your alerts stays focused on the strongest candidates without going stale.

## 8. Delivery

- Alerts are sent instantly to Telegram.
- Optional WhatsApp mirroring is available if you'd like every alert duplicated there too.
